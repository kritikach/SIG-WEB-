BTS

BTS, also known as the Bangtan Boys, is a South Korean boy band that was formed in 2010 and debuted in 2013 under Big Hit Entertainment. The septet—consisting of members Jin, Suga, J-Hope, RM, Jimin, V, and Jungkook—co-writes and co-produces much of their own output.

JIMIN

Park Ji-Min aka Jimin is one of the most active members of BTS on social media. Born on October 13 in 1995, Jimin spent most of his childhood in the Busan district of South Korea. With a strong affinity towards dancing from an early age, Jimin started learning popping-and-locking in middle school. Post which he joined the Big Hit Entertainment and started training under them. Meanwhile, Jimin is currently studying media studies with Jungkook, RM, Suga, J-Hope and V. Apart from being the group's main dancer, Jimin is also a vocalist with three solo tracks titled: Lie, Serendipity and Filter. The BTS ARMY is already in love with Jimin's infectious smile, but the talented singer continues to impress them with his creativity as a singer and a performer

Yoongi

Min Yoon-gi, better known by his stage names SUGA and Agust D is a South Korean rapper-songwriter and record producer under Big Hit Music. He is a rapper of BTS, also he is notable for his large input in songwriting and is the major music producer between the members.
